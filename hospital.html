What is Cashless Hospitalisation?

Cashless hospitalisation means that you do not have to pay any cash or have a financial transaction with the hospital directly. It enables you and your family to focus on treatment and recovery without worrying about the bills piling up. Within Cashless Mediclaim, all your medical expenses are borne by your insurance provider.

How Does Cashless Hospitalisation Work?

When availing cashless hospitalisation, be sure to check the network hospital list before getting admitted. This list contains the names of all the hospitals that your insurer has tied up with to provide cashless Mediclaim.

Benefits of Cashless Hospitalisation

Ease in Cash Burden: Cashless hospitalisation is particularly beneficial in emergency medical situations. With cashless Mediclaim, you can focus on treatment without any monetary worries.

Expansive Coverage: With MedInsure cashless Mediclaim, you are covered throughout the hospitalisation period with a dedicated Third Party Administrator (TPA) ever-ready to solve any concerns.

Tax Benefits: As health insurance is tax-exempt under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act 1961, you also enjoy tax benefits while ensuring top-of-the-line treatment cash-free.
